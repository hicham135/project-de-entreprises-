If Icons Don't Appear:

  • Check your internet connection (the CDN requires internet access).
  • Open the CDN link in your browser: https://cdnjs.cloudflare.com/ajax/libs/font-awesome/6.4.0/css/all.min.css. If it loads CSS, the CDN is active.
  • Disable ad-blockers/extensions (they sometimes block CDN requests).
  • Try another browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox).
